Passenger Van Mirrors

2019-2020

Led mechanical design for a new passenger van exterior auto-dimming mirror. This mirror has a curved construction to enable a wider field of view.

Development of a new exterior rearview mirror, launching an injection molded carriers, a laser-cut glass element, a heated adhesive foam, and a wire harnesses. This product also uses the BSM I developed here. The limiting design factor for this product was a draft angle that was not normal to the flat carrier plane. This resulted in modeling challenges.
