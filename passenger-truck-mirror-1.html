Passenger Truck Mirror

2019-2020

Led mechanical design for a new passenger truck exterior auto-dimming mirror. This was a LH only mirror development - many vehicles put a dimming mirror only on the driver side, as that is where the primary glare comes from.

Development of a new exterior rearview mirror, launching an injection molded carrier, two laser-cut glass elements, a heated adhesive foam, and a wire harness. This product also uses the BSM I developed here. This product and this product were coincident launches (one is for the standard variant of the truck, and one for the trailer tow variant). This resulted in a high workload for me - but still the same timeframe! As my first solo product launch, I had to learn fast and ask lots of questions.
For these launches, I led DFMEA reviews, conducted many tolerance analyses, and did iterative 3D printing to ensure the wire routing was clear and easy for manufacturing. Of particular interest to me during this project was learning about the design of the heated foam that is in between the glass and the plastic carrier. This heater melts ice on and/or defrosts the mirror within a matter of minutes, something I wish I had back in frigid Minnesota.
