Frameless Interior Mirror

2021-2022

Led mechanical design for a frameless interior auto-dimming mirror for multiple vehicles from various OEM's. Frameless mirrors have an exposed, polished edge (for aesthetic purposes).

CAD Images

Developed a new interior rearview mirror, launching an injection molded rear housing, two laser-cut glass elements, a die-cut foam tape, and a new PCB (printed circuit board). This product is an offshoot of the novel rearview mirror construction I developed here. Relative to that construction, this mirror's change points were glass resizing, an updated connection interface, an updated PCB locating scheme, and new customer/regulatory validation. These changes required a DFMEA, tolerance stack ups, thermal expansion analysis, and careful review of updated customer requirements.

In addition to the standard analyses above, the snap in the connection interface required static FEA to verify installation/retention force, as well as to confirm the stress limits of the resin material were not exceeded during install. I used CATIA FEA to verify and tune this, confirming the initial analysis with the company's FEA expert (through ANSYS). I also conducted early prototyping work to minimize risk of regulatory non-compliance which could have jeopardized the Gantt schedule. This product is currently undergoing final validation and manufacturing trials.
